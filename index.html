Women in Tech Vienna

Welcome to the Women in Tech Vienna community. We communicate primarily via Slack, so if you would like to join us, just read through the Code of Conduct and sign up.

Who are we?

We are a group of like-minded women, working in tech or trying to start out working in tech in the city of Vienna. We motivate each other, post about speaking opportunities, give each other feedback and join events and hackathons together.

If you are primarily identifying as a woman and are working, or interested in working in tech, feel free to join our merry little band.

Code of Conduct

We have a tiny set of rules we need everybody to comply with to keep this group nice and friendly.
